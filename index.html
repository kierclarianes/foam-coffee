Our story

It started with one good cup.

fōam began as two friends, one secondhand espresso machine, and a stubborn idea: that a daily coffee should feel like a small kindness, not a transaction.

We roast in small batches a few days before you drink them, dial in every shot by taste, and steam milk until it turns soft and glossy — the foam that gave us our name.

The cloud on our cups is smiling for a reason. We hope you do too, somewhere around the first sip.
